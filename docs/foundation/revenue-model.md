## 1. Pricing Strategy

HomeHero uses a **hybrid value-and-time pricing strategy** to ensure fairness for both customers and technicians:

$$\text{Total Invoice Amount} = \text{Base Booking Fee} + (\text{Hourly Rate} \times \text{Job Duration}) + \text{Material Costs} + \text{Surge Multipliers}$$

*   **Base Booking Fee**: Flat ₹399–₹599 (covers dispatch and standard diagnosis).
*   **Hourly Rate**: Standardized ₹199–₹399 per hour depending on trade complexity.
*   **Dynamic Surge Multipliers**: Surcharges of 1.2x to 1.5x applied during monsoons, holidays, or night shifts (10:00 PM – 6:00 AM).

## 2. Five Core Revenue Streams

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                  HOMEHERO REVENUE ENGINE                   │
├──────────────────────────────┬──────────────────────────────┤
│ 1. Transaction Commission    │ 15% fee on completed jobs    │
│ 2. Premium Hero visibility   │ Monthly subscription fee     │
│ 3. B2B Property contracts    │ Monthly retainer contracts   │
│ 4. Supply sponsorships       │ Hardware brand promotions    │
│ 5. Lead Generation listings  │ Lead selling for renovations │
└──────────────────────────────┴──────────────────────────────┘
```

### 2.1 Transaction Commission Model (Primary)
The platform charges a **15% commission** on the subtotal (base fee + hourly labor) of all completed jobs. Material costs are passed through directly to prevent price inflation disputes.

### 2.2 B2B Subscription Model
Offers residential complexes, co-living spaces, and property managers a subscription-based maintenance package:
- **Silver Tier (₹4,999/month)**: Up to 10 emergency dispatches/month.
- **Gold Tier (₹9,999/month)**: Up to 25 emergency dispatches/month + priority dispatching.

### 2.3 Premium Hero Visibility Plans
Technicians can subscribe to premium visibility packages to appear first in customer searches:
- **Basic (Free)**: Standard proximity-based dispatch matching.
- **Pro (₹999/month)**: Priority matching in search results, reduced platform commission (capped at 12%), and access to premium jobs.

### 2.4 Lead Generation for Renovations
For large, non-emergency projects (e.g. complete home rewiring, modular kitchen renovations), HomeHero acts as a lead generation engine, selling vetted customer project requests to local contractors for a flat fee of ₹250–₹500 per lead.

### 2.5 Supply Chain Sponsorship & Advertising
Partnerships with hardware and tool brands (e.g. Havells, Bosch, Asian Paints) to feature their products in our technician onboarding kits and run sponsored advertisements inside the app directories.

## 4. Break-Even Analysis

### 4.1 Monthly Fixed Costs (Year 1 Sandbox)
- **Engineering & Hosting (Render, Atlas, APIs)**: ₹1,50,000/month
- **Customer Support & Vetting Operations**: ₹1,20,000/month
- **Local Marketing & Acquisition**: ₹2,00,000/month
- **Administrative & Miscellaneous**: ₹80,000/month
- **Total Fixed Costs**: **₹5,50,000/month**

### 4.2 Break-Even Calculation
Given the average platform revenue per booking is ₹127.50 (15% of the average order value of ₹850):

$$\text{Monthly Break-Even Volume} = \frac{\text{Monthly Fixed Costs}}{\text{Average Commission per Booking}} = \frac{₹5,50,000}{₹127.50} \approx 4,314 \text{ bookings/month}$$

The platform achieves operational break-even when completing **4,314 bookings per month** (approximately 144 completed bookings per day in our target city).

## 5. Profitability & Margin Analysis

*   **Gross Margin**: Estimated at **82%** in Year 3. Variable service costs are primarily Google Maps API queries and SMS/Email gateway costs (calculated at ~₹22 per booking transaction).
*   **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) Payback**: With a CAC of ₹250 per customer, and an average order margin of ₹127.50, a customer needs to make **2 bookings** to pay back their acquisition cost, achieving a healthy LTV:CAC ratio of 4:1 by month 12.
