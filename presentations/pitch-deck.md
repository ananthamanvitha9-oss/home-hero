# HomeHero: Hyperlocal Home Services Platform (India)
*Pitch Deck & Investor Presentation Outline*

---

## Slide 1: The Vision
**HomeHero**  
*Bringing transparency, safety, and efficiency to India's hyperlocal home services.*

*   **Subtitle:** On-demand access to verified electricians, plumbers, carpenters, and appliance technicians at fair, upfront prices.
*   **The Hook:** Transforming a $10 Billion unorganized offline market into a standard, UPI-driven service marketplace.

---

## Slide 2: The Problem (The Indian Reality)
*   **For Customers:**
    *   *Arbitrary Pricing:* Techs negotiate arbitrary prices based on the customer’s house size or urgency.
    *   *The Trust Deficit:* Allowing unverified, random workers into the household causes high safety anxiety (specifically for women/seniors).
    *   *Extreme Unreliability:* 40%+ delay or no-show rates among independent local handymen.
*   **For Service Professionals (Heroes):**
    *   *Platform Exploitation:* Major incumbents (e.g., Urban Company) charge up to 30% commission, leading to worker strikes and churn.
    *   *Seasonality Dry-Spells:* AC/appliance techs face near-zero income in winters, while plumbers/electricians lack direct consumer outreach tools.

---

## Slide 3: The Solution (The HomeHero Ecosystem)
*   **Upfront Pricing Engine:** Dynamic, detailed estimators compute flat-rate quotes *before* booking checkout. No bargaining, no hidden fees.
*   **Verified Hero Network:** Multi-point background check (Checkr/Stripe ID checks) and liability coverage up to ₹50,000.
*   **Fair Take-Rates (15-20%):** Lower commission increases provider net earnings, leading to superior service quality and high retention.
*   **PostGIS Matching:** Real-time geofenced dispatch connects bookings to the closest available online Hero within a 15-mile operating radius.

---

## Slide 4: Market Opportunity
*   **Total Addressable Market (TAM):** USD **$10B - $12B** Indian home services market by 2026, growing at a **18-20% CAGR**.
*   **Emerging Blue Ocean (Tier 2/Tier 3):** Rapid urban growth in cities like Jaipur, Coimbatore, and Lucknow with rising disposable income and *zero* organized aggregator presence.
*   **UPI-Driven Adoption:** High mobile-payment penetration allows cashless, frictionless transactions in regional areas.

---

## Slide 5: Business Model & Monetization
*   **Transactional Commissions:** 15% on standard services (cleaning, handyman) and 20% on skilled trades (electrical, plumbing).
*   **Convenience Fee:** Flat ₹49 platform fee per booking to cover payment gateways and operational maps APIs.
*   **Hero+ Membership (AMC):** ₹999/year subscription for recurring clients, offering free annual home health checkups and waived service fees.
*   **Hardware / Lead Sales:** Commision margins from partnerships with local hardware shops for parts sourced by Heroes during jobs.

---

## Slide 6: Unit Economics & Payback
*   **Average Order Value (AOV):** ₹9,000 ($120.00 equivalent).
*   **Gross Margin per Job:** ~₹1,180 net commission.
*   **Target CAC:** ₹3,300 ($45.00) via local search SEO and referral loops.
*   **Customer Lifetime Value (LTV):** ₹14,800 ($200.00 equivalent) over 3 years.
*   **LTV : CAC Ratio:** **4.4x**, proving high long-term venture sustainability.

---

## Slide 7: Go-To-Market (GTM) Strategy
*   **Phase 1: Hyperlocal Launch**
    *   Onboard the first 100 "Founding Heroes" in a single test city by offering guaranteed weekly minimum earnings.
    *   Partner with local resident welfare associations (RWAs) to generate hyper-local trust.
*   **Phase 2: Referral Engine**
    *   Double-sided referral bonuses (₹200 to customer and ₹200 to referred friend).
    *   Multilingual interface launch (Hindi, Telugu, Tamil) to unlock regional "Bharat" customer segments.

---

*   **The Ask:** Seeking **$1.5M Seed Funding** to accelerate product development and initial expansion.
*   **Allocation:**
